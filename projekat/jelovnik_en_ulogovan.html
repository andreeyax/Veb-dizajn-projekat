Main Courses

Our selection of main courses offers a variety of flavors and authentic recipes that will satisfy even the most demanding gourmets.
Each portion is carefully prepared with the freshest ingredients to ensure top quality and an unforgettable experience.
Our main courses are the perfect way to experience the richness and diversity of Chinese cuisine, providing you with a meal that is both satisfying and memorable.
Chinese Spareribs
Chinese Spareribs are marinated ribs that are slowly cooked to perfect tenderness. Our ribs are enriched with traditional Chinese spices and a honey-soy glaze, giving them a unique sweet and savory flavor. Every bite provides true enjoyment.
Small portion: 750 din
Large portion: 1100 din
Mapo Tofu (Mapo Tofu)
Mapo Tofu is one of the most popular dishes in our cuisine, known for its spiciness and richness of flavor.
Small portion: 680 din
Large portion: 1020 din
Honey Walnut Shrimp
Honey Walnut Shrimp is a dish that combines the crispiness of shrimp, the richness of walnuts, and the sweetness of a honey glaze. Each bite is a harmony between juicy shrimp coated in a crispy crust, topped with a sweet and creamy honey-walnut glaze. This dish wins hearts.
Small portion: 720 din
Large portion: 1080 din
